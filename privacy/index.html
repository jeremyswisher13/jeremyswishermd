Privacy and analytics

Useful measurement, kept deliberately limited.

This independent professional website uses cookie-free analytics only to understand which public pages and scheduling links help visitors take the next step.

Last updated July 21, 2026

What is measured

Aggregate scheduling actions.

The site records an event when a visitor selects a phone link or the UCLA Health online appointment-request link. Each event contains the action type, the public page path, a broad link location such as navigation, hero, sidebar, content, contact, referral, or final call to action, and the time needed to count the event.

Automatic pageview collection is disabled. The analytics configuration also omits referrers, campaign parameters, country or time-zone estimates, session IDs, time on page, scroll depth, user-agent details, screen size, viewport size, and browser language.

What is not collected

No personal or clinical information.

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, or other contact details
  • Symptoms, diagnoses, medical-record information, insurance information, or form contents
  • The phone number selected, full destination URL, link text, URL query string, or page contents
  • Cookies, local-storage identifiers, advertising identifiers, or a profile of activity across pages or websites

This independent website does not provide a medical-message form, patient portal, or appointment form.

Analytics provider

Why Simple Analytics is used.

The site uses Simple Analytics for these limited events. Simple Analytics states that it does not store IP addresses, place cookies, fingerprint visitors, or create device identifiers. Optional analytics metrics are disabled. The provider processes essential technical fields plus the event name, public page path, and broad link location needed to count each action. Visitors whose browsers communicate Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control are not measured by this site.

UCLA Health services

Appointments and clinical communication remain with UCLA Health.

Selecting the UCLA Health appointment-request link takes you to a separate UCLA Health website. This independent site can count that the link was selected, but it cannot see the information submitted there. UCLA Health separately manages appointments, medical records, billing, and clinical communication under its own privacy practices.
